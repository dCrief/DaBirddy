### 1. Add your logo
Upload your logo image to this repo and name it **`logo.jpg`** (or update the `src` in `index.html` to match your filename).

### 2. Update your links
Open `index.html` and replace the following placeholders:

| Placeholder | Replace with |
|---|---|
| `YOUR_INVITE` | Your Discord invite code (e.g. `abc123`) |
| `YOUR_USERNAME` | Your Whatnot username |
| `YOUR_REFERRAL_CODE` | Your Whatnot referral code |
| `YOUR_HANDLE` | Your Instagram & TikTok handles |
| `@YOUR_VENMO` | Your Venmo username |
| `YOUR_PAYPAL` | Your PayPal.me slug |
| `YOUR_EMAIL_OR_PHONE` | Your Zelle email or phone number |

### 3. Update card shows
In `index.html`, find the show cards section and edit/add `.show-card` blocks with your upcoming show dates and locations.
